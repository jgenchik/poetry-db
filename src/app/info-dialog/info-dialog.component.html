About This App

Welcome to the poetry exploration app! Here, you can search for poems, read them, and listen to them through our text-to-speech feature.

Powered by PoetryDB

Our app utilizes the PoetryDB API, the world's first API for next-generation internet poets. This free and open-source API provides access to a vast collection of classic poetry. We extend our sincere gratitude to the creators of PoetryDB for making this resource available to the public.

Features

  • Search: Find poems by title, author, or keywords.
  • Read: Enjoy the full text of poems within the app.
  • Listen: Experience poems audibly with our text-to-speech functionality.
